Welcome to Anomaverse!

Your goal is to acquire all the items in your "Desired Items" list by bartering with other traders.

  • Connect: Start by connecting your wallet to get a unique identity and choose an avatar.
  • Create Intents: You can't trade directly. Select one item you HAVE and one you WANT, then click "Create Intent" to broadcast your wish to the network.
  • Run the Solver: Click "Run Solver"! This powerful engine looks at ALL active intents and automatically executes fair trades, even complex 3-way deals.
  • Win: The game is won when your inventory contains all of your desired items. Good luck, trader!
